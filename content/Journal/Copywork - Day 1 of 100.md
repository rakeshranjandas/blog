# Don't Shave The Yak

**Author** - Seth Godin

**Source** - https://seths.blog/2005/03/dont_shave_that/

### Summary

**Structure of the post**
```
Brief definition
Real-life example
It's worse when groups are involved
Why working solo is better
Remedy, don't get involved
```

**In own words**
In this world filled with distractions and our indecisiveness, the author describes about a phenomenon called "yak shaving", which mires our day-to-day activities. It is when a person starting out doing one thing finds himself doing a completely different thing. Although the intent of doing are connected with reason, no actual work in being done. To get out of this trap, the author advises to abandon at the exact moment you find you are deviating from real work.

### How I felt
- Can't memorise even a single sentence in full. I was oscillating from notepad to screen every word.
- Some sentences I couldn't understand until I reread for multiple times and tried to memorise. There were several of these "tubelight" moments, but when those happened, I felt glad thinking that at least I didn't miss out on what author really wanted to say.
