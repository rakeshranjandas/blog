# Quieting the Lizard Brain

**Author** - Seth Godin  
**Source** - https://seths.blog/2010/01/quieting-the-lizard-brain/

### Summary

```
Why are we so full of contradictions? Lizard brain or the resistance
Examples of resistance
Lizard brain is a physical part of brain
Company run by lizard brain
Our job is to ignore the lizard
```

The author attributes human's indecisiveness, resistance to change and contradictions to a physical part of a human's brain that has been there since pre-historic era, that he calls the "lizard brain". This part is responsible for fear and risks. Companies run by lizard brains fear risk-taking and thus are mediocre at best, and have no chance of competing with giants like Apple. This part of human will always be there. To do something worthwhile, we've to learn to ignore it and start taking bold decisions.
