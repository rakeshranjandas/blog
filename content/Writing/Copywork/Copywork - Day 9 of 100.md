# define: Brand

**Author** - Seth Godin  
**Source** - https://seths.blog/2009/12/define-brand/

### Summary

```
- Brand definition
- Design is not brand
```

The author says that a brand's value is something influences people to choose it over the alternatives. A brand does this through telling stories, setting expectations, leveraging memories or building relationships with the consumer. But most think of brand as just a logo or a design that showcases the product's beliefs or ambitions in some cryptic sense. Honestly people don't care much about that, they don't even see them. Capturing audience through emotional bonding with people is what a brand is supposed to be.
