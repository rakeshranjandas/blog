# First, ten

**Author** - Seth Godin  
**Source** - https://seths.blog/2009/04/first-ten/  


### Summary

```
- Market to first 10
- Receive feedback and improve on it
- Gradually building instead of a grand launch
```

In order to be successful, the author suggests to build a small, loyal and trustworthy audience first. He asks to find the "first ten" person who listen to you and give proper feedback that you can take to improve the service/product. If they like it, they could further spread it to more people they know. The business might appear slow and might even cost more at first. This requires patience and proper planning. The author says that the audience is more satisfied when one builds and releases gradually, instead of holding off things for a grand release.
