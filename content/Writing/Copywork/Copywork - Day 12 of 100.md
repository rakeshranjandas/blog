# Are You Making Something?

**Author** - Seth Godin  
**Source** - https://seths.blog/2011/03/are-you-making-something/


### Summary
```
- Work is something that has a lasting value and it needs focus.
- It's easy to confuse work with distraction. Examples.
- One reason for this confusion is when same device is used for work and recreation. Distraction comes along with productivity.
- Two-device solution - Separate the devices used for work and not work.
```

The author speaks about how distractions come along with productivity. When people use the same device, then work could be confused with "not work" and one might not realise until it's too late. Work requires focus. The author proposes a "Two- device" solution to help become aware of what's work and what's distraction. Using one device for work and work only, and another for not-work related things helps create separation and one gets conscious when he's choosing work or break.
