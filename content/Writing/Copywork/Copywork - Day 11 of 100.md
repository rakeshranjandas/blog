# The Modern Business Plan

**Author** - Seth Godin  
**Source** - https://seths.blog/2010/05/the-modern-business-plan/

### Summary

```
A proper business plan
- Truth
- Assertions
- Alternatives
- People
- Money
```

The author tries to describe what a modern business plan should look like and how adopting it could help convince stakeholders. The modern business plan should explain these 5 main points properly.
(1) Truth - Involves ground research on existing market conditions. The idea is to be clear of objectives and assumptions.
(2) Assertions - Explain the future plans and actions that you think will provide a desirable outcome.
(3) Alternatives - When (and almost always) assertions fail, what are the mitigation strategies. 
(4) People - What kind of people are and should be on the team
(5) Money - Deals with all the financial aspects like investment, margin, P&Ls, cash-flow etc.
