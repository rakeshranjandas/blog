# How To Be Remarkable

**Author** - Seth Godin  
**Source** - https://seths.blog/2007/01/how_to_be_remar/  


### Summary

```
1) Commit
2) Remarkable = worth making a remark
3) Being noticed is not same as being remarkable
4) Extremism is ok
5) Being at edge - fastest, richest, slowest etc.
6) Don't please everyone, but a few who can help you
7) First and Best
8) Masses are threatened not to be remarkable
9) Be remarkable at something people care about
10) Reinvest an reinvent
```

The author lists 10 ways on how to become remarkable at something. Being first and best at anything gives you enough popularity, so much so that the next or the runners up don't matter. Remarkable people constantly learn from their mistakes, redesign their strategy and, most importantly, commit. They are people of action. Instead of attempting to please the general masses, they cater to the needs of a selective audience and rely on them to make them popular. Lastly, remarkable people try their best to stay remarkable by improvising, reinventing and reinvesting, instead of being stagnant.
