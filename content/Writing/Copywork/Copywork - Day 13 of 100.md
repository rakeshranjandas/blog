# Hard work vs Long work

**Author** - Seth Godin  
**Source** - https://seths.blog/2011/05/hard-work-vs-long-work/


### Summary
```
- What's long work and what's hard work.
- Hard work is efficient, but has risk associated with it
- Long work sets the stage for hard work
```


The author describes how long work and hard work actually work. Long work is putting up hours. It's required sometimes, but it doesn't always bring actual change, as often these are low risk and low reward. Hard work is when one considers the risk and uses his skill and wits to get the job done in very less time. People often choose long work over hard work because of the risk involved. But real change is brought by people doing hard work, and these people are more celebrated and have more value because not everyone can do their job.
